Eco Wordle Battle: Player vs AI

Instructions:

You and the AI each have a secret 5-letter eco-themed word to guess. Take turns making guesses. The colors will show:

  • Green - Correct letter in correct position
  • Yellow - Correct letter in wrong position
  • Gray - Letter not in the word

The winner is whoever guesses their word in fewer attempts. You have a maximum of 6 guesses each.
